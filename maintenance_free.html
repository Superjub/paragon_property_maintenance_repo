At Paragon Property Maintenance:

All of our UPVc products are manufactured to the highest quality using an A rated energy efficient glass with security locks which are made by one of the leading manufacturers in the country. The windows and doors used are specifically designed and manufactured for Park Home use.


Using the "odd-leg" UPVc double glazed system specially designed for Park Home use , we offer a straightforward "like for like" service, whereby we will replace the old windows or doors with new windows or doors of the same dimensions, or alternatively we can alter the existing apertures of your home enabling you to alter the sizes of your new windows or doors, or replace existing doors and windows for french or patio doors.


PPM install low maintenance windows and doors and they come in a range of colours to suit you. We can transform the look of your home by changing flat windows to bay windows (or vice versa) or add french or patio doors where your exisiting living room window is to bring the outside in, all using our low maintenance UPVc products.
