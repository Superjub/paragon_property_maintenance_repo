Paragon Property Maintenance Ltd are one of the countries Park Home Refurbishment specialists, but did you know that unlike many refurbishment companies we also specialise in interior refurbishments. Whether you are considering a change of decoration or a full internal refurbishment PPM can help and advise you. We offer a complete and comprehensive internal refurbishment service that includes painting and decorating, carpentry, electrical, plumbing and heating through to the design and installation of kitchens, bathrooms, wetrooms and bespoke bedroom furniture.

Our interiors team can cover every aspect of work concerning the inside of your Park Home including:

  • Essential re-panelling or flooring due to flood or fire damage. Working in conjunction with customers and alongside your insurer we are able to assist every step of the way in the event of anything happening to your home.We are able to oversee the contract right from the survey and damage assessment stage through to the quote and ultimately carrying out the work with the minimum amount of fuss or disruption to customers, giving you peace of mind!


  • Our Plumbing and Heating engineers can install new boilers or kitchen appliances, the moving or adding of new radiators or simply advice on ventilation.


  • Our electricians, with years of experience in the Park Home industry, can help with any electrical issues, from moving or adding power points (inside or out), installing new lighting, upgrading of consumer units and anything else concerning your homes electrics.


  • Whether you are thinking of a complete change of layout, re-boarding the internal walls or ceilings of your home, the upgrading or widening of internal doors or simply a change of decoration, our carpentry and decorating team can help to transform your home.
