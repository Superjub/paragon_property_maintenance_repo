Paragon Property Maintenance Ltd are a Northamptonshire based company situated right at the very heart of the Park Home industry. With over 25 years experience within the company, we believe that when it comes to Park and Holiday Homes we can be relied upon to give the appropriate help and advice needed. We pride ourselves on offering a professional service, and strive to work closely and considerately with customers to carry out our contracts with the minimum amount of disruption.


We offer superior workmanship that is second to none, and to offer an extensive range of services, both internally and externally, from full refurbishments to minor repairs. Using the considerable wealth of skills and experience shared by our team we are able to deal with virtually any issues that arise, from essential Re-Panelling and Roofing, through to internal requirements such as Kitchens and Bathrooms, and anything inbetween.


PPM are ideally situated to cover contracts throughout the entire network of UK Residential and Holiday Home Parks. We offer a free no obligation quotation and all of our work is guaranteed, as are our products, as we use the best quality materials that are themselves used and recommended by the leading Park and Holiday Home manufacturers.
